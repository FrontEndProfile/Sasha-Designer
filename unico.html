Unico

UX/UI, Web

IDEA & TASK

This app invites the user to choose content that can improve his or her mood. In addition, the user creates a personal profile and his own collection of books, music, etc. And he can also track changes in his emotions over the course of a week or month.

RESEARCH

Before I started working on the design, I did research: competitive analysis and in-depth interviews with both the target audience and business representatives. I prepared a JTBD, put together a CJM and an information architecture for the site. This helped to better identify the basic needs of users and significantly shorten the user's path to the final goal.

USER PROFILE

The user's main journey begins with selecting the right diet for him and ends with placing and paying for the order. But the key issue was the decision to make the user profile as convenient and functional as possible. With the ability to adjust menus, delivery dates and the option of ordering meals for other family members.
