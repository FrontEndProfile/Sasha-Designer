Moody

UX/UI, App, Concept

IDEA & TASK

This app invites the user to choose content that can improve his or her mood. In addition, the user creates a personal profile and his own collection of books, music, etc. And he can also track changes in his emotions over the course of a week or month.

RESEARCH & UX

This app invites the user to choose content that can improve his or her mood. In addition, the user creates a personal profile and his own collection of books, music, etc. And he can also track changes in his emotions over the course of a week or month.

UI Elements

The color scheme was chosen in contrasting, but pleasing to the eye, cool hues. An akzidenz grotesk was used for the titles. The style of the illustrations is as simple and concise as possible. The easy giff animation of the illustrated characters nicely complements the overall concept.

Logic

The app has the ability to select the user's mood as well as entertainment content (books, movies, articles, etc.). The user can track his state statistics and save the content he likes. The app can be monetized through paid access to content and advertising.
