Main Partner

UX/UI, Web

IDEA & TASK

This app invites the user to choose content that can improve his or her mood. In addition, the user creates a personal profile and his own collection of books, music, etc. And he can also track changes in his emotions over the course of a week or month.

UI ELEMENTS

Main Partner already had a corporate identity and my job was to use the brand guidelines, giving them a fresh new solution, but without losing the company's recognition for their clients. Based on the analysis of competitors and the client's wishes, for the covers of the main pages as a metaphor, I chose the technique of dynamically moving 3D figures, making together a single field.

LOGIC

Corporate website consists of four main sections: About Us, Services, Media, Contacts. The structure is quite simple and intuitive. It makes it easy to navigate for new users.
